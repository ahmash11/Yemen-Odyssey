Trip 1: Socotra, Hadramout & Aden – The Ultimate Adventure

This trip takes you through three of Yemen’s most fascinating and diverse regions: the stunning island of Socotra, the ancient desert cities of Hadramout, and the lively coastal city of Aden. Experience nature, culture, and history in the most unique and unforgettable way.

  • Duration: 10 Days
  • Best Time to Visit: September to March (cooler weather)
  • Price: $1500 (Per Person)

Trip 2: The Heart of Yemen – Sana'a, Taiz, and Ibb

This trip offers a deeper look into Yemen’s heartland and its rich cultural heritage. From the historic Old City of Sana’a to the mountainous beauty of Taiz and Ibb, this journey brings you face-to-face with ancient cities, stunning landscapes, and vibrant local culture.

  • Duration: 7 Days
  • Best Time to Visit: Year-round
  • Price: $1200 (Per Person)

Trip 3: Desert Adventure – Jawf and Marib

For those looking to explore the vast, sweeping deserts of Yemen, the Desert Adventure Trip takes you through the mesmerizing landscapes of Jawf and Marib. Experience life in the desert, discover the ancient ruins of Marib. This trip is perfect for adventurers and history lovers alike, offering a unique look at Yemen’s desert wonders and historical gems.

  • Duration: 6 Days
  • Best Time to Visit: October to March (milder desert temperatures)
  • Price: $1000 (Per Person)

Trip 4: Explore All of Yemen – A Journey Through Yemen’s Diverse Regions

This is the ultimate trip for those looking to experience everything Yemen has to offer. From the vast deserts of Marib and Saadah, to the lush mountains of Ibb and Taiz, to the historic heart of Sana’a, and the coastal charm of Aden—this trip covers all of Yemen’s regions. Get ready for an adventure like no other.

  • Duration: 14 Days
  • Best Time to Visit: September to March (cooler weather)
  • Price: $2500 (Per Person)
